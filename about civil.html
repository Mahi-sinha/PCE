Department of Civil Engineering

The Department of Civil Engineering is one of the core departments of the institute and functions under Bihar Engineering University (BEU), Patna. The program is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of infrastructure development and sustainable engineering practices.

With a strong foundation in structural design, construction technology, geotechnical analysis, and environmental engineering, the department nurtures analytical thinking and practical skills to tackle societal and environmental challenges.

The curriculum encompasses vital areas like transportation engineering, water resources, building materials, surveying, and smart infrastructure systems. Emphasis is placed on practical training, research projects, and fieldwork to ensure students are well-prepared for industry roles and higher studies.
