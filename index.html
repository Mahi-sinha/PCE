B.Tech Branches Offered

Explore the dynamic and in-demand engineering streams shaping the future.

Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)

Focuses on coding, algorithms, artificial intelligence, web & app development, and software systems.

Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Specializes in machine learning, data science, robotics, and intelligent automation technologies.

Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)

Deals with circuits, embedded systems, communication networks, and signal processing.

Mechatronics Engineering

Combines mechanical, electronics, robotics, and control systems for smart automation.

Electrical Engineering (EE)

Studies power systems, electrical machines, control systems, and sustainable energy solutions.

Civil Engineering

Focuses on structural design, construction techniques, environmental and transportation engineering.
