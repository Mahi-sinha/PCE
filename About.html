History

Purnea College of Engineering, Purnea is a Government Engineering College established in 2017. The inaugural ceremony was held on 17th February 2019 by the Honorable Chief Minister of Bihar, Shri Nitish Kumar.

It is affiliated to Bihar Engineering University (BEU), Patna and governed by the Department of Science and Technology, Patna, Bihar.

The institute offers B.Tech programs in:

  • Civil Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Mechatronics Engineering
  • Computer Science & Engineering
  • Computer Science & Engineering (Artificial Intelligence)

Mechanical and Mechatronics Engineering have an intake capacity of 30 students, while the other courses have an intake capacity of 60, all approved by AICTE, New Delhi.

Vision

Consistently strive for excellence in engineering education by producing skilled, trained, and knowledge-driven engineers who fit into the current and future requirements of industries and organisations, and thereby contribute to the growth of the country.

Mission

  • To improve the teaching–learning process.
  • To make the existing curriculum more contemporary and industry-relevant.
  • To create an environment for fostering research and development.
  • To develop in students soft skills, communication abilities, ethical values, leadership qualities, and analytical thinking.
  • To address the needs of neighbouring areas through engineering and technical support.

Administration

Administration and Management: Purnea College of Engineering is a full-fledged Government Institution under the Department of Science, Technology, and Technical Education, Government of Bihar, and functions accordingly. However, in academic and related matters, it actively engages with the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and other apex bodies like the Indian Society for Technical Education and Technical Teachers' Training Institutes to ensure innovation in pedagogical and practical aspects of technical education.

The Administration and Management of the institute are aligned with these objectives.

Although the Principal holds the overall charge of all institutional operations to ensure smooth functioning and efficient administration, various service departments are led by Professors with proven capabilities and experience.

Affiliation

The Institute is academically governed by Bihar Engineering University (BEU), Patna, which is the degree-awarding authority for the B.Tech. programs offered here. It is recognized by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, and the Government of Bihar.
