Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering

The Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering is a key department of the institute and operates under Bihar Engineering University (BEU), Patna. The program is designed to provide students with a thorough understanding of electronics and communication technologies.

Our focus is on the design, analysis, and implementation of electronic circuits, communication systems, and signal processing techniques. Students are encouraged to think creatively while mastering core concepts like analog and digital electronics, communication theory, and embedded systems.

The curriculum encompasses a diverse set of subjects, including VLSI design, wireless communication, signal processing, and embedded systems. The department also emphasizes practical training, research, and project-based learning to ensure well-rounded development.
