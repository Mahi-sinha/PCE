Department of Mechatronics Engineering

The Department of Mechatronics Engineering is an interdisciplinary department within the institute, operating under Bihar Engineering University (BEU), Patna. The program is structured to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of mechanical, electrical, computer, and control systems.

Our focus lies in the synergistic integration of these engineering disciplines to design and develop intelligent and automated systems. Students are encouraged to think holistically and innovatively while mastering core concepts like robotics, automation, sensors, and control systems.

The curriculum includes a wide spectrum of subjects: mechanical design, electronics, control theory, microprocessors, and robotics. The department also emphasizes hands-on learning, research, and project-based implementation to ensure students are well-prepared for the challenges of modern engineering.
